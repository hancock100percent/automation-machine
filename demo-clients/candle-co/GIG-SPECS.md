## PRIMARY GIG: AI Marketing Automation System

### Gig Title
"I will build an AI marketing automation system for your business"

### Gig Description

```
Tired of spending hours on content creation? Let AI do the heavy lifting.

I'll build you a COMPLETE marketing automation system that:

✅ Generates unlimited social media posts tailored to your brand voice
✅ Creates professional product images and graphics automatically
✅ Schedules and posts content across platforms
✅ Tracks performance with a custom analytics dashboard
✅ Runs 24/7 without your involvement

THIS IS NOT just AI-generated content. This is a SYSTEM that keeps working for you.

**What you get:**

BASIC ($500):
- Brand voice training (AI learns your style)
- Content generation system (text posts)
- 30-day content calendar generated
- Setup documentation

STANDARD ($1,200):
- Everything in Basic
- AI image generation integration
- Auto-posting to 2 platforms
- Basic analytics dashboard
- 1 month of support

PREMIUM ($2,500):
- Everything in Standard
- Full multi-platform automation
- Advanced analytics & reporting
- Custom workflow modifications
- 3 months of support
- Priority response

**Perfect for:**
- E-commerce brands scaling their social presence
- Content creators who want to post consistently
- Agencies managing multiple client accounts
- Anyone tired of the content hamster wheel

**My tech stack:**
- Local AI models (no per-query costs for you)
- ComfyUI for image generation
- Custom orchestration system I built myself
- Integrations with major platforms

Message me with your brand and goals - I'll tell you exactly what I can build.
```

### Pricing Rationale

| Tier | Price | Margin | Time Investment | Value Proposition |
|------|-------|--------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Basic | $500 | ~80% | 4-6 hours | Entry point, proves concept |
| Standard | $1,200 | ~75% | 10-15 hours | Full system, most popular |
| Premium | $2,500 | ~70% | 20-30 hours | Enterprise-ready, high touch |

**Why these prices:**
- Automation systems on Fiverr range $200-$5,000
- $500 entry is accessible but filters tire-kickers
- Standard is the target conversion tier
- Premium exists for serious buyers and anchoring

### Portfolio Requirements

1. **Case Study: Candle Co.**
   - 30-day content calendar (generated)
   - Sample product images (from ComfyUI)
   - Dashboard screenshot
   - Before/after metrics (mocked but realistic)

2. **System Demo Video**
   - Show the orchestration in action
   - Demonstrate content generation
   - Show image creation
   - Brief dashboard walkthrough
   - 2-3 minutes max

3. **Screenshots**
   - Content calendar view
   - Generated images grid
   - Dashboard with charts
   - Code/system architecture (builds trust)
