## Production Notes

### SadTalker Avatar Settings

- **Source:** Your photo (well-lit, front-facing, neutral expression)
- **Audio:** Your voice recording (clear, confident, quiet room)
- **Preprocess:** crop (focus on face)
- **Enhancer:** gfpgan (face enhancement)
- **Expression scale:** 1.0 (natural movement)
- **Output:** 25fps MP4 with synced audio

### Screen Recording Guidelines

- Resolution: 1920x1080
- Clean desktop (hide icons)
- Use dark terminal theme for contrast
- Slow, deliberate mouse movements
- Pause on important elements (2-3 seconds)

### Canva Design Specs

- **Dimensions:** 1920x1080 (landscape for Fiverr)
- **Brand colors:** Match candle-co warm tones
- **Font:** Clean sans-serif (Montserrat or similar)
- **Transitions:** Smooth dissolves, no flashy effects

### ComfyUI Video Settings

**Image-to-Video (Wan2.1):**
- **Model:** wan2.1_i2v_480p_14B
- **Motion prompt:** "gentle flame flicker, soft shadows, warm glow"
- **Duration:** 81 frames (~3.4s @ 24fps)
- **Output:** MP4, 24fps

**Talking Head (SadTalker):**
- **Model:** SadTalker_V0.0.2_512
- **Preprocess:** crop
- **Enhancer:** gfpgan
- **Output:** MP4, 25fps with audio

## Audio Recording Guide

### Equipment
- Any decent microphone (USB mic, phone, headset)
- Quiet room (no echo, no background noise)
- Close to mic (6-12 inches)

### Recording Tips
1. Record each segment separately
2. Leave 1 second silence at start/end
3. Speak clearly, slightly slower than normal
4. Keep energy up but professional
5. Do multiple takes, pick best one

### Audio Files to Record

| File | Duration | Script Segment |
|------|----------|----------------|
| v1_intro.wav | ~8s | "What if AI could plan..." |
| v1_outro.wav | ~15s | "30 posts. Custom images..." |
| v2_cta.wav | ~10s | "Professional AI images..." |
| v3_intro.wav | ~5s | "See exactly how..." |

Save audio files to: `demo-clients/candle-co/audio/`

## Export Settings

**Final Video Export:**
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Resolution: 1920x1080
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Bitrate: 10-15 Mbps
- Audio: AAC, 192kbps

**Thumbnail Export:**
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Resolution: 1280x720 (Fiverr standard)
- File size: Under 2MB
